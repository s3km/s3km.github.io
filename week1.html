My Study Abroad Journey - Week 1

In the first week of my study abroad program in Germany, I applied my knowledge of HTML and CSS to real-world scenarios. It was an exhilarating experience to see my skills in action!

In the electrical workshop, I had the opportunity to learn how to mount an electrical outlet and install smart home light switches. With the guidance of our tutor, I gained hands-on experience that brought my classroom knowledge to life.
